Our Story

How it all started.

The vision began while living in suites at Bentley University. We were very neat guys but no one EVER wanted to be the one to clean the bathroom. We dreamt of a service that would come in and clean just our bathroom but our only option was to pay a minimum of $60 and have our entire dorm room cleaned. After researching available options, we came to the conclusion that we should be the ones to provide this cleaning service. We have found that there is really a need for people to have a single room cleaned while not having to have their entire places cleaned.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine the cleaning industry to give the masses access to a convenient, flexible and cost-effective way of having their cleaning needs met.

Our Vision

Create a fantastic, one-of-a-kind service that provides customers with a cleaner for less than the standard minimum of $50 by offering an area-specific cleaning service.
